## Before you start

You need to be logged in as an **Org Owner**. Organisation settings are not accessible to tutors or students.

## Steps

### 1. Open organisation settings

From the sidebar, click **Settings**, then select the **Organisation** tab (or navigate directly to `/settings/org`).

### 2. Fill in your organisation details

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Your tutoring business name (shown to students, parents, and on invoices) |
| **Logo** | Upload a square PNG or JPG — recommended 400×400 px |
| **Timezone** | Your local timezone — this is used for all scheduling |
| **Website** | Your public website URL (optional) |

### 3. Add subjects

Click **Subjects** and select every subject your org offers. Tutors will later pick which of these they teach, and students will be enrolled in subject-specific classrooms.

Common subject groupings:
- Maths (Primary, Junior, Senior, VCE Methods, VCE Specialist)
- English (Primary, Junior, Senior, VCE)
- Sciences (Chemistry, Physics, Biology)
- Languages, Music, Test Prep

### 4. Save

Click **Save changes**. Your org name and logo will appear immediately across the platform — in the sidebar, on invoices, and on your public booking page.
