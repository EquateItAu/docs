## Overview

ClassQuill sends automatic reminders before every booked session. No setup required — reminders go out as long as the session is confirmed and the recipient has a valid email address.

---

## What gets sent and when

| Reminder | Sent to | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Email reminder | Parent, student, tutor | 24 hours before |
| Push notification (app) | Parent, student, tutor | 1 hour before |
| SMS | Parent (if mobile number on file) | 24 hours before |

<Note>
  SMS requires a mobile phone number on the parent or student's account. Go to **Settings → Profile** to add or update a phone number.
</Note>

---

## What the reminder contains

- Session date, time, and duration
- Tutor name
- Session format (in-person address or video call join link)
- Link to the session in the app

For video sessions, the join link is included directly in the reminder email — no need to log in to find it.

---

## If a session is rescheduled or cancelled

When a session is rescheduled or cancelled, ClassQuill immediately sends a notification to all parties with the updated details. A fresh reminder goes out 24 hours before the rescheduled session.

---

## Turning off reminders

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Toggle **Session reminders** on or off per channel (email / push / SMS)

Individual users control their own notification settings — org admins cannot disable reminders for a specific user.
