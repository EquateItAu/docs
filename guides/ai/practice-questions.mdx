## The Question Bank

The Question Bank is a searchable library of practice questions organised by subject, topic, and difficulty. Access it via **Question Bank** in the sidebar.

---

## Finding questions

| Filter | Options |
|---|---|
| **Subject** | Maths, English, Chemistry, Physics, etc. |
| **Topic** | Subtopics within each subject (e.g. Calculus → Differentiation → Chain Rule) |
| **Difficulty** | Easy / Medium / Hard |
| **Type** | Multiple choice / Short answer / Extended response |
| **Source** | Platform questions / Your org's own questions |

Click any question to preview the full text, expected answer, and marking scheme before assigning.

---

## Assigning questions

1. Select the questions you want to assign (tick the checkbox next to each).
2. Click **Assign**.
3. Choose the recipient — a classroom, an individual student, or a group.
4. Set an optional due date.
5. Click **Confirm**.

The student(s) receive a notification and the assignment appears in their **Home** screen.

---

## Saving questions during a session

Students can save questions themselves while studying:

1. The student finds a question they want to revisit.
2. They press the **Save** button (bookmark icon).
3. The question is added to their **Saved Questions** list.

The tutor can see all saved questions in the **Teaching** view before the next session — a ready-made queue of what the student was stuck on, with no prep needed.

---

## Creating your own questions

1. Go to **Question Bank** → **My Questions** tab.
2. Click **New question**.
3. Choose the type, enter the text, add images or LaTeX maths, and set the marking scheme.
4. Tag it with subject and topic.
5. Click **Save** — available to all tutors in your org.
