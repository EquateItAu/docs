## For parents

### Viewing your child's progress

1. Log in to the parent portal and go to **Home**.
2. Click on a tutor card.
3. Select the **Progress** tab.

You'll see:
- **Weakness areas** — topics where the student consistently struggles
- **Topic coverage** — which parts of the syllabus have been covered in sessions
- **Homework completion** — how much of assigned homework was completed on time
- **Time studied** — total study time logged in the app this week and month
- **Session summaries** — tutor-written notes from each completed session

### Viewing a formal progress report

When a tutor or admin has generated a formal report and shared it with you:

1. Go to the tutor card → **Progress** tab.
2. Scroll to **Reports**.
3. Click the report to open it or download the PDF.

---

## For tutors

### Seeing your student's weaknesses before a session

1. Go to **Teaching** and open the classroom or the student's 1:1 page.
2. Click the **Progress** or **Weaknesses** tab.
3. Review the AI-identified weakness areas and saved questions from last session.

### Generating a progress report

1. Open the student's page via **Teaching** → classroom → student.
2. Click **Generate report**.
3. Set the date range and add any written commentary.
4. Click **Send to parent** to share it directly, or **Download PDF** to save it.

---

## For org admins

1. Go to **Analytics** in the sidebar.
2. Filter by tutor, classroom, subject, or date range.
3. Click any student row to drill into their individual report.
