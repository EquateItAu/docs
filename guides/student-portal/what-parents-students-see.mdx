## The parent portal

Parents log in at [app.classquill.com](https://app.classquill.com) and land on their **Home** page, which shows all tutors linked to their child.

### What parents can access

| Section | What's there |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Tutors linked to their child — click any tutor to see sessions, chat, and homework |
| **Calendar** | Upcoming and past sessions; book, reschedule, or cancel |
| **Messages** | Direct messaging with tutors |
| **Billing** | All invoices, payment history, PDF receipts |
| **Find Tutor** | Browse and book tutors in the org |
| **Settings** | Profile, phone number, notification preferences |

### What parents can see about their child

- Upcoming and completed sessions (dates, tutors, duration)
- Session summaries written by the tutor after each lesson
- Homework assigned to their child and completion status
- Progress reports (topic coverage, weakness areas, time studied)
- Messages between the child and tutor (read-only view)

<Info>
  Parents cannot see the student's AI chat conversations — those are private to the student.
</Info>

---

## The student portal

Students see a different interface tailored to learning.

### What students can access

| Section | What's there |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Learning Hub — quick links to active homework, saved questions, and AI tools |
| **Study** | Practice questions filtered by subject and topic |
| **Ask AI** | AI study assistant — ask questions, upload photos of problems |
| **Calendar** | Upcoming sessions and homework due dates |
| **Messages** | Chat with their tutor |
| **Progress** | Weakness analytics, topic coverage, time studied |
| **News** | Org announcements and updates |

### What students cannot do

- Book sessions themselves (parents or admins book on their behalf)
- See other students' data
- Access billing or payments

---

## Messaging is contained

All messaging happens within ClassQuill. Tutor ↔ student and parent ↔ tutor messaging is separate — no cross-over, no random DMs from outside the platform.
